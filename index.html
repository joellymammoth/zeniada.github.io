Submit

Seeking submissions for our Spring 2019 issue. All submissions are judged anonymously.

Eligibility

Zeniada accepts poetry (including prose poetry) and artwork. Anyone who is enrolled in an undergraduate program for the current school year is welcome to submit.


Guidelines

Email your submissions to submit@zeniada.com.

1/ The subject of your email should be either "Poetry" or "Art."

2/ In the body of the email, include your: name, graduation year, school, and any notes regarding your work.

3/ Poetry should be attached as a Word (or other editable) document. Do not include your name, school, or any other identification.


Questions

Any questions regarding submissions can be sent to ask@zeniada.com.
