News/Emails


Wed Jan 11

Hi everyone,

Hope your break went/is going well! Just a couple of things as we head into our Spring 2017 semester:

1. This spring, we'll be meeting in the radio station (located in McCoy). Please fill out this poll so we can find a time that hopefully works for everyone (meetings will be weekly, like last semester, and most likely around an hour long).

2. We're sending our Fall 2016 issue to print as soon as funding and such permits. In the meantime, the magazine is now available online on our website (under "Past Issues"). If you notice anything that needs to be changed (or that you think should be changed), definitely let me know!

3. Our Facebook event for Spring 2017 is up; deadline is Sunday, February 12. Feel free to invite friends and peers from all universities--it'd be great if our next issue could actually be intercollegiate. And of course, feel free to submit!

4. Last semester was our first time getting the magazine up and running in the past few years, so there's obviously a lot of room for improvement. Please fill out this survey if you'd like to offer comments/suggestions/etc. about editorial meetings and the overall publishing process.
